TROUBLESHOOTING QUICK FIXES

Problem:              Solution:
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Robot not visible     → Check WebGL support (F12 console)
Voice not working     → Unmute speaker, check browser permissions
Forms not saving      → Verify SQL tables exist in Supabase
No data in database   → Refresh page, fill form, wait 5 seconds
404 errors            → Wait 30 secs for deployment to finish
Domain issues         → Wait 1-2 hours for DNS propagation
